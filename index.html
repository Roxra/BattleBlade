BattleBlade

BattleBlade is a 2D Fighter game with Bullet Hell elements featuring a story-based campaign about Gods and Demons fighting for Earth. Read visual novel style dialogue to discover the truth! BattleBlade also features a local multiplayer versus mode allowing you to battle your friends!
